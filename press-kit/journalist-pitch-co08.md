## Talking Points for Follow-Up Calls

**"Why CO-08 specifically?"**
> "It's one of the most competitive districts in the country — Cook rates it EVEN. There's already a documented pattern of constituents wanting engagement and not getting it. It felt like the right place to start."

**"Is this targeting Republicans?"**
> "No. We launched in CO-08 because the constituent engagement story was already active there. We have profiles on all 537 federal officials. If a Democrat has a 0% response rate, it shows the same way. The platform is nonpartisan by design."

**"What if Evans never uses the platform?"**
> "That's kind of the point. The platform works without politician participation. If qualifying questions pile up and he doesn't respond, his response rate stays at 0%. That's news in itself — and it's permanent."

**"How is this different from Change.org or a petition site?"**
> "Petitions go into the void. WhyTho creates a permanent, weekly public record. It's not about getting one moment of attention — it's about building a score over time that follows a politician through their career."

**"Is the AI-generated content a concern?"**
> "Every AI-generated question is clearly labeled '📋 Suggested Question — AI-generated from public record.' AI analysis is labeled separately and never presented as a statement from the politician. We're very deliberate about that."
